    -You can send your package at Kerry Express Parcel shop, Kerry Express parcel service point and partner brands. Find your Kerry Express Service Point
    -You can also request pickup service via Kerry Express Application Android or IOS

    -Standard Shipping Service: 1-2 Days Sameday Service (Priority Service): Within 1 Day AM Service: Next Day before Noon. For more details please contact our Call Centre 1217

    -You can check Kerry Express Shipping Rates through 3 channels:
    1.Kerry Express Application Android or IOS
    2.Kerry Express Live Chat
    3.Kerry Express Call Centre 1217

    -4 Channels to request pickup service
    1.Kerry Express Application: Choose Ship and click Pickup request. (Click to Download Kerry Express Application LinkedAndroid or IOS)
    2.Kerry Express LINE Official Account: Go to rich menu and choose pickup request (Click to add Kerry Express Offical Line )
    3.Kerry Express Easyship Website : Login into your Kerry Express Account then choose pickup request
    4.Call Centre: Call 1217 and request for pickup service with agent.
    * Please pack and prepare all packages prior courier arrival for pickup.

    -Yes, Kerry Express service point provides parcel sachels and parcel boxes.

    -Customer can register for COD service through 2 channels:Kerry Express Website Kerry Express Application Android or IOS
    To register a COD account, please prepare and submit following documents during registrations.
    For individual registration
    1.Copy of Identification Card
    2.Certificate of Name Change (if any)
    3.Copy of Bank Passbook
    For Corporation/Legal Entity
    1.Copy of Company Affidavit
    2.Copy of Identification Card of authorized person and attorny
    3.Power of Attorny (if any)
    4.Copy of Bank Passbook

    -Kerry Express will send COD summary report via email once the parcel is delivered. Or you can check the updates at EasyShip Website

    -Kerry Express will remit the COD amount into your bank after we delivered the package and collects the COD amount from Consignee.
    -For Bangkok Bank account, Kerry will remit 3 working days after we have delivered the package and 4 working days for other banks.

    -COD Service Rate is 3% of COD amount to be collected.
    Ex. If COD amount is 1,000 baht, the COD fee will be 30 Baht.
    *No additional charge for bank transfers.
