    -3 Channels to track your package:
    1.Kerry Express Website: Tracking Page
    2.Kerry Express Appplication Android or IOS
    3.Kerry Express LINE Offical Account

    -Customer can check estimated delivery date through our shipment tracking in Kerry Express Website, Kerry Express Application( Android or IOS ) or Kerry Express LINE Offical Account. Below is an example where you can find the estimate delivery date in our tracking

    -Our courrier deliver shipment prioritize by estimated delivery date. Customer might receive shipment before estimate delivery date in case courrier can deliver after finish delivery on due shipment.

    -Customer can contact origin Kerry Express parcel shop or our call centre 1217 to request for address or phone number change if the parcel have not been delivered. Additional shipping rates will applied in case of delivery address change.

    -In case of incorrect consignee contract number, customer can contact Kerry Express origin parcel shop or our call centre 1217 to change phone number if the parcel has not been delivered.

    -All parcels are scheduled for delivery in accordance to its shipping service and estimate delivery date. Our courrier will contact to reciever before delivery. Customer can postpone delivery date on call or contact our call centre 1217 to postpone delivery date.

    -If sender would like to cancel shipment, please contact origin Kerry Express parcel shop or our call centre 1217

    -Following Kerry Express process, our courier will try to contact consignee again and reschedule the delivery to next day.

    -Following Kerry Express process, our customer service will contact the sender to correct the contact number. Then our courier will schedule the shipment for delivery next day. Likewise, Consignee/Sender can also contact our call centre 1217 to update shipment delivery information.

    -Following Kerry Express process, our customer service will re-confirm delivery address with sender. In case of incorrect delivery address customer may ask to return shipment back to sender or request to change delivery address. Delivery adress changing fee will be applied Customer can contact origin Kerry Express parcel shop or our call centre 1217 to change delivery address.

    -For more details on late/delayed shipment please contact our call centre 1217 or our live chat on our website

    -You can pick up returned packages at the origin Kerry Express parcel shop or contact our call centre 1217.
