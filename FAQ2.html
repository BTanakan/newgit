    • Shipment Claims and Liability Coverage

    -Standard liability for lost and damaged shipment is maximum 2,000 THB per consignment. Customer can check our liability at https://th.kerryexpress.com/en/our-services/terms.php

    -Customers can purchase our Extra Care Service (additional liability coverage) at any Kerry Parcel Shop, starting from 10 THB for maximum liability 5,000 THB per consignment. Maximum extension of liability up to 50,000 per consignment. The details

    -In case of any lost and damaged shipment, Sender can fill out the claim request form and submit claim request form to our Claim Centre by email TH.EX.Claim@kerrylogistics.com within 14 days after received shipment.
    Please prepare and submit following documents;
    1.Claim request form
    2.invoice / receipt**
    3.Copy of Identification card or passport (only foreigner)
    4.Company affidavit// Vat Registeration(PP.20) (for any request on behalf of Company)
    5.Pictures of damaged shipment, including packaging (In case of damage)
    Note: ** Copy of invoice, receipt, confirmation with a screen (In case of online ordering) with proof of payment, etc.

    -Please contact our call centre 1217 press 5 on your claim process or through our live chat on our Kerry Express Website
